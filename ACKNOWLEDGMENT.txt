VULNERABILITY DETAILS:

Type: Broken Link Hijacking (BLH)
Platform: BBC News Website (instagram.com)
Severity: Medium (CVSS 5.4)
Status: Successfully Remediated

DISCLOSURE TIMELINE:

Report Submitted: 15-01-2026
Initial Response: 15-01-2026
Vulnerability Fixed: 05-06-2026
Public Acknowledgment: 09-06-2026

IMPACT:

Before Remediation:
- Broken Instagram link pointing to abandoned account
- Abandoned account username available for re-registration
- Potential for brand impersonation
- Risk to audience of misinformation exposure

After Remediation:
- ✅ Official BBC Instagram account secured
- ✅ Verified status (blue checkmark) applied
- ✅ All external links updated and validated
- ✅ Brand monitoring implemented
- ✅ Preventive measures in place
